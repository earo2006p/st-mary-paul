Liturgical Calendar

Upcoming Church Feasts

Major feasts and observances on the Coptic calendar over the next three months.

July 12, 2026

Feast of the Apostles

Commemorating the martyrdom of Saints Peter and Paul, marking the end of the Apostles' Fast.

Aug 7 – 21, 2026

Fast & Vigil of the Virgin

Two weeks of fasting and evening prayers (Nahda) in honor of St. Mary, leading up to her feast.

Aug 19, 2026

Feast of the Transfiguration

Remembering Christ's transfiguration on Mount Tabor before Peter, James, and John.

Aug 22, 2026

Feast of the Assumption of St. Mary

Celebrating the assumption of the body of the Virgin Mary, closing her two-week fast.

Sept 11, 2026

Coptic New Year (Nayrouz)

The beginning of the Coptic calendar year, honoring the martyrs of the Church.

Sept 27, 2026

Feast of the Holy Cross

Commemorating the finding and elevation of the True Cross.
