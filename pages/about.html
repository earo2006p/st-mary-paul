Church Identity

St. Mary & St. Paul Church

The church bears the blessed names of St. Mary and St. Paul the Apostle. Its life is centered on worship, sacramental life, pastoral care, family discipleship, youth formation, and practical service.

The church lives the spirit of worship, reverence, service, and joyful fellowship, drawing strength from the words of St. Paul: “I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me.”

Our Journey

Parish Milestones

Year of Founding
Church Founded
The church was established in the name of St. Mary and St. Paul the Apostle, and the first Divine Liturgy was celebrated in a simple gathering of faithful families.
Church Building
Church Consecrated
The current church building was consecrated, marking a defining milestone in the parish's history and providing a permanent home for worship and sacramental life.
Sunday School
Sunday School Launched
A formal Sunday School program was established to nurture the faith of children and youth, growing steadily to serve dozens of families every week.
Iconostasis
Iconostasis Installed
A beautifully crafted traditional iconostasis was installed in the main sanctuary, enriching the liturgical beauty and reverence of the church.
Pope's Visit
Papal Visit
His Holiness Pope Tawadros II honoured the church with a pastoral visit, strengthening the bond between the parish and the Coptic Orthodox Church.
Today
A Growing Parish Family
The church continues to grow in worship, service, and discipleship — a vibrant community rooted in the Orthodox faith and open to all who seek Christ.
